Our Commitment to Privacy

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide our IP detection service. We do not store your personal information, create user profiles, or sell data to third parties. Your privacy is our priority.

Information We Collect

When you use What's My IP?, we automatically collect the following information:

  • IP Address: Your public IP address (IPv4 and/or IPv6)
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location based on IP geolocation
  • Connection Information: Internet Service Provider (ISP) and network details
  • Browser Information: User agent string, browser type, and operating system
  • Technical Data: Timestamp of visit, request headers, and response codes

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Displaying your IP address and connection details
  • Providing geolocation services
  • API functionality for developers
  • Security monitoring and abuse prevention
  • Service performance optimization
  • Generating anonymous usage statistics

Data Retention

We follow minimal data retention practices:

  • IP Addresses: Not permanently stored; only kept in temporary server logs
  • Server Logs: Maintained for 30 days for security and performance monitoring
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained longer for service improvement
  • API Usage: Rate limiting data is cleared every 24 hours

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Limited data sharing occurs only in these situations:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law enforcement or legal proceedings
  • Service Providers: Anonymous data with hosting and security providers for operational purposes
  • Security Issues: When necessary to investigate abuse or security threats

Third-Party Services

Our service may use these third-party components:

  • IP Geolocation APIs: For location detection (data is not stored by us)
  • CDN Services: For faster content delivery (minimal logging)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (no personal identification)

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Theme Preference: Stores your dark/light mode choice locally
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use advertising or tracking cookies
  • No User Accounts: No registration required, no login cookies

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we may have
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure and regular updates
  • Limited access to server logs and data
  • Regular security monitoring and threat detection
  • Incident response procedures for security breaches

International Data Transfers

If you access our service from outside our server location, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
