Introduction

At Jaiho TechServe (a subsidiary of Jaiho Digital), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the "What's My IP?" tool and API.

Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. When you visit our website:

  • IP Address: Your IP address is processed momentarily to display it to you. We do not store this in a database or sell it to third parties.
  • Browser Information: We detect your browser and operating system type to display it on your screen. This happens locally or via standard HTTP headers.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use standard web technologies to improve your experience:

  • Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to remember your preferred theme (Dark Mode or Light Mode).
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use invasive tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

API Usage

If you use our API, we may momentarily process your request to return the data. We do not maintain a permanent log of API requests associated with specific users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
